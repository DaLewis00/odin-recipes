Margarita

The Margarita is one of the most popular cocktails in North America and around the world. It combines the tang of lime and the sweetness of orange liqueur with the distinctive strength of tequila, the classic Margarita strikes all of the right keys.

--Ingredients--

  • 2 ounces of tequila blanco
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce orange liquor
  • 1/2 agave syrup
  • Garnish: lime wheel
  • Garnish: salt

--Instructions--

  1. Add tequila, orange liqueur, lime juice and agave syrup to a cocktail shaker filled with ice, and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel and kosher salt rim (optional).
