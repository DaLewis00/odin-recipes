Guacamole

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip, spread, or salad originally from Mexico. In addition to its use in modern Mexican cuisine, it has become part of international cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient.

--Ingredients--

  • Ripe avocados
  • Tomatos
  • Cilantro
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic

--Instructions--

  1. Slice three ripe avocados in half, remove the pit and scoop them into a mixing bowl.
  2. Use a fork to gently mash them to your desired level of chunky or smooth, (you can use a latex cooking glove to smash with your hand if you're feeling adventurous!).
  3. Add the onions, tomatoes, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, garlic, lime juice and salt and stir everything together.

Enjoy!
